Russell's Bar Mitzvah

A Celebration of Faith, Family, and Coming of Age

Mazel Tov Russell

Russell has been eagerly preparing for his Bar Mitzvah and can't wait to celebrate with everyone. His journey has been one of growth, learning, and inspiration.

In addition to his studies, Russell is passionate about:

  • playing basketball with his Hoopcode club team
  • competing in flag football for his school
  • attending shul on Shabbat
  • spending time with his friends

He is excited to share this special simcha with family and friends as he steps into his role as a full member of the Jewish community.
